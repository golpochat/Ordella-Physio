# Logo System

## Primary Logo
- Wordmark: "Ordella Physio"
- Typeface: Inter / geometric sans-serif
- Weight: SemiBold
- Letter spacing: -1%
- Style: Clean, modern, minimal

## Logo Mark
- Geometric symbol representing:
  - precision
  - alignment
  - care
  - movement
- Shape: abstract "O" with motion arcs

## Logo Usage Rules
- Minimum clear space: height of "O"
- Minimum size: 24px height
- Do not stretch, distort, recolor, or rotate

## Color Variants
- Primary: #4F46E5
- White
- Black
- Gradient: #4F46E5 → #6366F1
