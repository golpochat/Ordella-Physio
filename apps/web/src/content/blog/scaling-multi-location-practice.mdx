Opening a second location is exciting — and challenging. Without unified software, each site develops its own processes, making reporting and billing a nightmare for headquarters.

Multi-location support requires centralized patient records, role-based access per site, and consolidated reporting. Ordella's Clinic plan is designed specifically for groups managing 2–10 locations.

The clinics that scale successfully invest in systems early. Don't wait until location three to fix your tech stack.
