Automated reminders are the single most effective tool for reducing no-shows. Clinics that send both email and SMS reminders 24–48 hours before appointments see up to a 30% reduction in missed sessions.

Beyond reminders, consider implementing a clear cancellation policy, offering easy online rescheduling, and following up with patients who miss appointments. Ordella Physio bundles all of these workflows into one platform.

Start with one change this week — even a simple reminder sequence can make a measurable difference to your bottom line.
