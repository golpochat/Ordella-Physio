SOAP notes — Subjective, Objective, Assessment, Plan — provide a consistent framework for documenting every patient encounter. When your whole team uses the same structure, handoffs between therapists become seamless.

Digital SOAP notes also create an audit trail that protects your clinic during insurance reviews and regulatory inspections. Ordella's note templates are customizable per clinic while maintaining clinical best practices.

If you're still using paper or unstructured digital notes, migrating to SOAP format is one of the highest-ROI changes you can make this quarter.
