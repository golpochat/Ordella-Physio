## Community Challenges

### Quarterly challenge program

| Quarter | Challenge | Reward |
|---------|-----------|--------|
| Q1 | **Setup Sprint** — 100% onboarding in 7 days | 1 month free (random draw) |
| Q2 | **Note Ninja** — most SOAP notes with AI review | $100 gift card |
| Q3 | **Patient Champion** — highest portal adoption | Featured case study |
| Q4 | **Integration Hero** — connect 3 marketplace apps | Pro upgrade 3 months |

### Challenge mechanics

1. Announce in community + email
2. Track via product events (automated leaderboard)
3. Weekly progress emails
4. Winners announced live on webinar
5. Winners → referral advocates

### Participation targets

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Challenge enrollment | 20% of active clinics |
| Completion rate | 40% of enrollees |
| Referral from participants | 2× vs non-participants |
