## Features Section

### Section headline
**Everything your clinic needs. Nothing it doesn't.**

### Section sub-headline
Purpose-built for physiotherapy — not a generic EMR squeezed into your workflow.

---

### Feature 1: Smart scheduling
**Headline:** Appointments that work for therapists and patients

**Body:** Create, manage, and cancel appointments across your clinic. Therapists set availability. Patients see upcoming visits in their portal. Sync with Google Calendar through the integrations marketplace.

**Bullet points:**
- Multi-therapist calendar views
- Patient self-service appointment visibility
- Automated appointment notifications
- Google Calendar sync (Marketplace)

---

### Feature 2: Clinical documentation
**Headline:** SOAP notes with AI assistance — therapist-approved, always

**Body:** Draft clinical notes faster with AI-generated SOAP summaries, treatment plan suggestions, and voice-to-note transcription. Every AI output requires therapist review and acceptance before it is saved.

**Bullet points:**
- Structured SOAP note templates
- AI-assisted drafting with preview modal
- Voice-to-note (Whisper transcription)
- Mandatory therapist review gate

---

### Feature 3: Patient engagement
**Headline:** A portal and mobile app your patients will actually use

**Body:** Give patients a modern experience — view appointments, receive notifications, message their clinic, and access care information from web or mobile.

**Bullet points:**
- Dedicated patient portal
- Flutter mobile app (iOS & Android)
- In-app messaging and push notifications
- Clean, English-only interface

---

### Feature 4: Billing & payments
**Headline:** Get paid without the paperwork

**Body:** Manage subscriptions, generate invoices, and accept payments through Stripe. Clinic admins see billing status at a glance. Patients access invoices through the portal.

**Bullet points:**
- Stripe subscription management
- Invoice generation linked to appointments
- Customer billing portal
- Plan upgrades (Starter → Pro → Enterprise)

---

### Feature 5: Reporting & insights
**Headline:** See how your clinic is performing

**Body:** KPI dashboards, async report generation, and CSV/JSON exports give clinic owners and managers the visibility they need to make decisions.

**Bullet points:**
- Role-based report access
- Appointment, revenue, and activity reports
- Downloadable exports
- Super Admin cross-clinic visibility (operators)

---

### Feature 6: Integrations marketplace
**Headline:** Connect the tools you already use

**Body:** Enable Google Calendar, Stripe, Twilio, SendGrid, Dropbox, Zoom, and exercise platforms from a single admin panel. No custom development required.

**Bullet points:**
- 10 integration providers
- OAuth and API key connection flows
- Per-tenant enablement
- Usage logging and health visibility

---

### Feature 7: Enterprise-ready
**Headline:** Scale to clinic groups with confidence

**Body:** SSO, custom roles, audit logs, API keys, and signed webhooks — built for organizations that need security, compliance, and control.

**Bullet points:**
- SAML 2.0, Azure AD, Google Workspace SSO
- RBAC v2 with custom roles
- Audit and activity logs
- Multi-region deployment (EU, US, APAC)
