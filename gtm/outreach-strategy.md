## Cold Call Script (Optional)

**Duration:** 3–5 minutes  
**Best time:** Tuesday–Thursday, 9–11 AM or 2–4 PM local

### Opening
> "Hi, is this [First Name]? … Great. My name is [Your Name] from Ordella Physio. I'll be brief — do you have 3 minutes?"

*If no:* "No problem. When's a better time — tomorrow morning or afternoon?"

### Hook
> "We work with physio clinics that are tired of juggling scheduling, notes, and billing across different tools. We built a single platform specifically for physiotherapy — I wanted to see if that's something [Clinic Name] is dealing with."

### Discovery (pick one)
> "How are you handling clinical notes today — paper, Word, or a software tool?"

> "How many therapists are you running now?"

### Value statement
> "Ordella gives every role its own portal — therapists, patients, and admins. We've got AI-assisted SOAP notes where the therapist always reviews before saving. And billing runs through Stripe."

### CTA
> "Would a 15-minute demo make sense this week or next? I can show you the therapist workflow and billing in one session."

*If yes:* Book demo, confirm email, send calendar invite.  
*If not now:* "Can I send you a 2-page overview and a trial link? No follow-up calls unless you want them."

### Close
> "Thanks for your time, [First Name]. I'll send that over shortly. Have a great day."

## Outreach Compliance

- **GDPR / CAN-SPAM:** Include unsubscribe link in all marketing emails. Honor opt-outs within 48 hours.
- **LinkedIn:** Respect connection limits (100/week). No automated spam tools.
- **Cold call:** Respect Do Not Call lists. Call clinic business lines only.
- **Healthcare:** Never claim clinical outcomes. AI messaging must include therapist review qualifier.
- **English-only:** All outreach in English. Do not promise localization.
